## Overview

The **UrbanLume Smart Facade Lighting** system transforms Putrajaya's iconic landmarks into dynamic visual displays through connected architectural lighting technology. This system enables remote management of lighting themes, colors, and schedules for key city landmarks.

## Coverage

### Featured Landmarks
- **Seri Wawasan Bridge** - Iconic cable-stayed bridge
- **Putrajaya Lakeside** - Waterfront promenade
- **Landmark Buildings** - Selected government buildings
- **Public Spaces** - Key gathering areas

## Technology

### Smart Control
- Centralized management platform
- Remote scheduling and programming
- Real-time status monitoring
- Energy consumption tracking

### LED Technology
- High-efficiency LED fixtures
- RGB color mixing capability
- Long lifespan components
- Weather-resistant design

## Special Features

### Event Themes
- National celebration colors
- Festival-specific themes
- International event recognition
- Custom color programming

### Scheduling
- Automatic on/off timing
- Seasonal adjustments
- Special occasion overrides
- Energy-saving modes
