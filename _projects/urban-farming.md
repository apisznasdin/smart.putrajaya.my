## Overview

The **Smart Precision Urban Farming** pilot project demonstrates Putrajaya's commitment to sustainable urban agriculture and food security. Located on the rooftop of Kompleks Perbadanan Putrajaya, this hydroponic greenhouse showcases how smart technology can enable efficient food production within urban environments.

## Technology

### Hydroponic System
- Nutrient Film Technique (NFT) growing beds
- Deep Water Culture (DWC) tanks
- Vertical growing towers
- Automated nutrient dosing
- pH and EC monitoring
- Water recycling and filtration

### IoT Sensor Network
- Temperature and humidity sensors
- Light intensity monitors
- CO2 level detection
- Soil/water moisture sensors
- Nutrient concentration tracking
- Plant growth cameras

### Climate Control
- Automated ventilation system
- Evaporative cooling
- Shade cloth deployment
- LED supplemental lighting
- Heating elements for cold periods
- Humidity regulation

## Smart Features

### Real-Time Monitoring
- Web-based dashboard
- Mobile app access
- Alert notifications
- Historical data analysis
- Growth tracking
- Yield predictions

### Automation
- Scheduled irrigation
- Nutrient injection
- Climate adjustments
- Lighting schedules
- Harvest reminders
- Maintenance alerts

## Crops Grown

The pilot facility produces a variety of vegetables and herbs:
- Leafy greens (lettuce, spinach, kangkung)
- Herbs (basil, mint, coriander)
- Microgreens
- Cherry tomatoes
- Cucumbers
- Chili peppers

### Environmental

- No soil degradation

- Year-round production
- No pesticides required
- Local food production
