## Overview

The **MyAV Pilot with eMooVit** is a groundbreaking autonomous vehicle initiative that brings self-driving electric shuttles to Putrajaya's public roads. This pilot program positions Putrajaya as Malaysia's first city to test Level 4 autonomous vehicles in real-world conditions, marking a significant milestone in the nation's smart mobility journey.

## Project Significance

### Malaysia's First
- First public road AV testing in Malaysia
- Pioneer city for autonomous mobility
- National testbed for AV regulations
- Knowledge hub for AV development

### Smart City Leadership
- Demonstrates commitment to innovation
- Attracts technology investments
- Builds local expertise
- Positions for future mobility trends

## Safety Framework

### Multi-Layer Safety System

The pilot prioritizes safety through multiple measures:

#### Technology Safety
- Redundant sensor systems
- Fail-safe mechanisms
- Emergency stop capabilities
- Continuous system monitoring

#### Human Oversight
- Trained safety operator on every trip
- Manual override capability
- Real-time monitoring from control centre
- Emergency response protocols

#### Operational Safety
- Designated routes with controlled conditions
- Speed limitations during pilot
- Weather-dependent operations
- Regular vehicle inspections

### Safety Statistics Target
- Zero serious incidents
- Comprehensive incident reporting
- Continuous safety improvement
- Public confidence building

## Pilot Routes

### Current Coverage

The pilot operates on designated routes within Putrajaya:

- **Route A**: Putrajaya Sentral Loop
  - Connects transport hub to government precincts
  - Approximately 3 km circuit
  
- **Route B**: Precinct 1 Circuit
  - Serves Prime Minister's Office area
  - Internal precinct connectivity

### Route Features
- Dedicated lanes where possible
- Controlled intersections
- Clear signage and markings
- Safe boarding/alighting points

## Operational Details

### Service Hours
- Monday to Friday: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
- Weekends: 10:00 AM - 4:00 PM
- (Hours may vary based on pilot phase)

### How to Ride

1. **Approach**: Go to designated AV stop
2. **Wait**: Shuttle arrives on schedule
3. **Board**: Door opens automatically
4. **Ride**: Enjoy the autonomous experience
5. **Alight**: Exit at your destination stop

### Fare
Currently **FREE** during pilot phase to encourage public participation and feedback.
