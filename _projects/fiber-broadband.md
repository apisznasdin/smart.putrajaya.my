## Overview

**Wired Broadband (Fiber) Ready** ensures that Putrajaya maintains comprehensive fiber optic internet infrastructure, providing high-speed connectivity to all residential and commercial premises through the city's unique Common Utility Tunnel (CUT) system.

## Infrastructure

### Fiber Network
- Fiber-to-the-Premise (FTTP) coverage
- Multiple service provider options
- Gigabit-capable connections
- Redundant network paths

### Common Utility Tunnel
Putrajaya's unique underground infrastructure:
- Protected cable routing
- Easy maintenance access
- Future expansion capacity
- Minimal surface disruption

## JENDELA Alignment

Part of the national JENDELA (Jalinan Digital Negara) initiative:
- 100% premises passed target
- Quality of service standards
- Affordability requirements
- Speed tier availability

## Coverage

### Residential
- All precincts covered
- Apartment complexes
- Landed properties
- New developments

### Commercial
- Office buildings
- Government premises
- Business districts
- Industrial areas

## Service Providers

Multiple ISPs available:
- TM Unifi
- Maxis Fibre
- Time Fibre
- Other licensed operators
