## Overview

The **PICC AV System Upgrade** represents a significant modernization of the Putrajaya International Conference Centre's audio-visual infrastructure, transitioning to IP-based technology for enhanced flexibility and performance.

## Technology Upgrade

### AV-over-IP
- Network-based audio/video distribution
- High-definition content delivery
- Low-latency transmission
- Flexible routing capabilities

### Coverage
- Plenary Hall integration
- Conference Hall connectivity
- Meeting room systems
- Common area displays

## Capabilities

### Video Distribution
- 4K resolution support
- Multi-screen synchronization
- Real-time switching
- Recording capabilities

### Audio Systems
- Digital audio routing
- Multi-language interpretation
- Distributed sound systems
- High-fidelity reproduction

## Benefits

### For Events
- Seamless content sharing
- Professional presentation quality
- Flexible configuration
- Reliable operation

### For Operations
- Centralized management
- Remote diagnostics
- Simplified maintenance
- Scalable infrastructure

## PICC Facilities

The upgrade enhances:
- **Plenary Hall** - 3,000 seat capacity
- **Conference Halls** - Multiple configurable spaces
- **Meeting Rooms** - Executive facilities
- **Exhibition Areas** - Display integration
