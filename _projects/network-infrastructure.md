## Overview

The **4G & 5G Infrastructure** initiative ensures Putrajaya remains at the forefront of digital connectivity, providing the backbone for smart city applications, IoT deployments, and high-speed mobile services.

## Infrastructure Components

### 5G Network
- Digital Nasional Berhad (DNB) deployment
- Single Wholesale Network model
- Ultra-low latency connectivity
- High bandwidth capacity

### 4G Enhancement
- Expanded LTE coverage
- CRAN (Centralized RAN) deployment
- DRAN (Distributed RAN) infrastructure
- Capacity upgrades

### Tower Infrastructure
- Monopole installations
- Small cell deployments
- Building-mounted equipment
- Street furniture integration

## JENDELA Initiative

Part of the national JENDELA (Jalinan Digital Negara) plan:
- 100% 4G coverage target
- 5G rollout acceleration
- Fixed broadband expansion
- Quality of service improvements

## Smart City Applications

### IoT Support
- Sensor network connectivity
- Smart meter communications
- Environmental monitoring
- Traffic management systems

### Emergency Services
- Enhanced public safety communications
- First responder connectivity
- Crisis management support
- Real-time coordination

### Economic Enablement
- Digital business support
- Remote work capability
- E-commerce infrastructure
- Innovation ecosystem

## Partners

- **MCMC** - Regulatory and coordination
- **DNB** - 5G wholesale network
- **Telcos** - Retail services
- **Perbadanan Putrajaya** - Local coordination

## Future Plans

- Expanded 5G coverage
- Edge computing deployment
- Private 5G networks
- Advanced IoT applications
