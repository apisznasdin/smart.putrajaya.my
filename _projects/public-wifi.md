## Overview

**Free Public WiFi** initiative provides complimentary wireless internet access across Putrajaya's public spaces, ensuring digital connectivity is accessible to all residents and visitors.

## Coverage Areas

### Parks & Recreation
- Taman Botani Putrajaya
- Taman Putra Perdana
- Taman Wetland
- Lakeside promenade
- Recreational zones

### Public Facilities
- Major gathering spots
- Event venues
- Public transport hubs
- Government service areas

## Features

### Easy Access
- Simple connection process
- No registration required for basic access
- Clear usage guidelines
- Multi-device support

### Connectivity
- High-speed internet
- Reliable coverage
- Adequate bandwidth
- Regular maintenance

## Benefits

### Digital Inclusion
- Free access for all
- Bridging digital divide
- Educational support
- Economic enablement

### Visitor Experience
- Tourism information access
- Navigation support
- Social media sharing
- Communication facility

### Smart City Support
- IoT connectivity backbone
- Public safety integration
- Emergency communication
- City information access

## Partners

- **Telekom Malaysia (TM)** - Infrastructure provider
- **Perbadanan Putrajaya** - Management and coordination
- **MCMC** - Regulatory support

## Usage Guidelines

- Fair usage policy applies
- Content filtering for public safety
- No illegal activities
- Respect for other users
