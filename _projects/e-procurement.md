## Overview

The **Online Procurement System** (Sistem Perolehan) digitizes Putrajaya's procurement processes, enabling contractors and vendors to access tenders, purchase documents, and submit bids electronically.

## Features

### Tender Management
- Online tender announcements
- Digital document distribution
- Electronic bid submission
- Automated deadline tracking

### Vendor Services
- Company registration
- Document downloads
- Payment integration
- Status notifications

## Process Flow

### For Contractors
1. Register as vendor
2. Browse available tenders
3. Purchase tender documents
4. Prepare bid submission
5. Submit electronically
6. Track evaluation status

### For PPj
1. Publish tenders online
2. Manage inquiries
3. Receive submissions
4. Conduct evaluation
5. Award notification

## Benefits

### Transparency
- Public tender visibility
