## Overview

The **Panic Button System** provides Putrajaya residents and visitors with immediate access to emergency assistance through strategically placed intercoms directly connected to the Putrajaya Command Centre (PCC).

## How It Works

### Activation
1. Press the panic button
2. Immediate connection to PCC
3. Two-way voice communication enabled
4. Video feed activated for verification
5. Help dispatched to location

### Response Protocol
- PCC operators assess situation
- Appropriate responders dispatched
- Continuous monitoring until resolved
- Follow-up and documentation
