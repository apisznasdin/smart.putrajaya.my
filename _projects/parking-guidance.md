## Overview

The **Parking Guidance System** is a comprehensive solution deployed across Putrajaya to help drivers locate available parking quickly and efficiently. The system consists of two main components: electronic display boards at strategic locations and integration with the Putrajaya Mobile app.

## Electronic Display Boards

LED variable message signs are strategically located at key entry points including Dataran Putrajaya, displaying real-time information about:
- Available parking slots by zone
- Directional guidance to nearest available parking
- Special event parking notifications
- Alternative parking recommendations

## Mobile Integration

The system is integrated within the **Putrajaya Mobile** app, allowing users to:
- View parking availability remotely before arriving
- Plan their journey based on parking availability
- Receive notifications about parking status
- Navigate to available parking areas

## Benefits

### For Drivers
- Reduced time spent searching for parking
- Less fuel consumption and emissions
- Better journey planning
- Stress-free parking experience

### For the City
- Reduced traffic congestion
- Lower carbon emissions
- Better space utilization
- Enhanced visitor experience

## Coverage

The system covers major areas including:
- Dataran Putrajaya
- Alamanda Shopping Centre vicinity
- Government office complexes
- Major event venues
