## Overview

The **Putrajaya Command Centre (PCC)** serves as the centralised nerve centre for Smart Putrajaya, integrating various city systems and enabling real-time monitoring of urban operations. This state-of-the-art facility brings together multiple agencies and stakeholders to ensure seamless coordination in managing Putrajaya's smart city infrastructure.

## Mission

To provide a unified platform for city-wide monitoring, coordination, and response management, enabling data-driven decision-making and enhancing the quality of life for Putrajaya's residents and visitors.

## Core Functions

### Real-Time Monitoring
The PCC operates 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, providing continuous surveillance and monitoring of critical city infrastructure. Operators have access to live feeds from hundreds of CCTV cameras strategically positioned throughout Putrajaya.

### Multi-Agency Coordination
The centre facilitates seamless collaboration between various agencies including:
- Perbadanan Putrajaya
- Royal Malaysia Police (PDRM)
- Fire and Rescue Department
- Ministry of Health
- Traffic Management Authorities

### Emergency Response
In case of emergencies, the PCC serves as the primary coordination hub, enabling rapid response through:
- Instant communication with first responders
- Real-time incident tracking
- Resource allocation and deployment
- Public notification systems

### Data Analytics
Advanced analytics capabilities allow the centre to:
- Identify patterns and trends in city operations
- Predict potential issues before they escalate
- Optimize resource allocation
- Generate reports for decision-makers

## Technology Infrastructure

The PCC is equipped with cutting-edge technology including:

- **Video Wall Display**: Large-scale visualization system for comprehensive city overview
- **Geographic Information System (GIS)**: Spatial data management and mapping
- **Integrated Communication System**: Multi-channel communication platform
- **Data Management Platform**: Centralized data storage and processing
- **Business Intelligence Tools**: Advanced reporting and analytics

## Impact

Since its establishment, the PCC has significantly improved:

- **Response Times**: Faster emergency response through coordinated dispatch
- **Public Safety**: Enhanced surveillance and crime prevention
- **Traffic Management**: Better traffic flow through real-time monitoring
- **Service Delivery**: Improved coordination of city services

## Future Development

The PCC continues to evolve with plans to incorporate:
- Artificial Intelligence for predictive analytics
- Internet of Things (IoT) sensor integration
- Enhanced citizen engagement platforms
- Advanced data visualization technologies
