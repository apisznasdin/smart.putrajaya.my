## Overview

The **San-i Sense Smart Toilet** system represents a breakthrough in public facility management, using IoT sensors to monitor cleanliness, usage patterns, and consumable levels in real-time.

## Technology

### Sensor Network
- **Usage Counters** - Track visitor frequency
- **Odor Sensors** - Detect air quality issues
- **Consumable Monitors** - Soap, tissue, water levels
- **Floor Sensors** - Detect spills and wetness

### Auto-Clean System
- Triggered cleaning cycles
- Scheduled sanitization
- Emergency cleaning alerts
- UV disinfection capability

## Monitoring Dashboard

Facilities managers can access:
- Real-time status of all facilities
- Usage statistics and patterns
- Maintenance alerts
- Cleaning schedule compliance
- Historical performance data

## Benefits

### For Users
- Cleaner facilities
- Better maintained amenities
- Consistent service quality
- Reduced wait times

### For Management
- Optimized cleaning schedules
- Reduced complaints
- Data-driven decisions
- Resource efficiency

## Pilot Deployment

Currently deployed at select public toilets with plans to expand based on pilot results:
- Public parks
- Government buildings
- Event venues
- Transportation hubs

## Integration

Connected to central management system for:
- City-wide facility oversight
- Performance benchmarking
- Maintenance coordination
- Quality assurance
