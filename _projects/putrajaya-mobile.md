## Overview

The **Putrajaya Mobile App** serves as the official "Super App" for the city, providing residents and visitors with a comprehensive platform for accessing city services, making payments, reporting issues, and staying informed about city developments.

## Key Features

### E-Services
- License applications
- Permit renewals
- Facilities booking
- Service requests
- Document submissions

### Payments
- Bill payments
- Parking fees
- Event tickets
- Service charges
- Multiple payment methods

### Emergency Services
- Quick emergency reporting
- Direct line to PCC
- Incident photo upload
- Location sharing
- Status tracking

### Information Hub
- City news and announcements
- Event calendar
- Service alerts
- Weather updates
- Traffic information

## Integration

### Connected Services
- Parking Guidance System
- SISPAA complaints
- Facilities booking
- Payment gateway
- PCC emergency line

### Smart City Ecosystem
- Real-time data feeds
- Push notifications
- Location-based services
- Personalized content
