## Overview

The **Cashless Parking System** modernizes Putrajaya's parking infrastructure by enabling multiple digital payment methods, eliminating the need for physical coins and reducing queues at payment machines.

## Payment Methods

### Touch 'n Go
- Tap-and-pay convenience
- Wide acceptance across Malaysia
- Auto-reload compatible

### Card Payments
- Debit card acceptance
- Credit card support
- Contactless payment enabled

### E-Wallets
- DuitNow QR integration
- Multiple e-wallet support
- Instant transaction confirmation

## Benefits

### Convenience
- No need to carry coins
- Multiple payment options
- Quick transaction processing
- Digital receipt generation

### Efficiency
- Reduced queuing time
- Automated payment processing
- Real-time transaction records
- Lower maintenance costs

### Security
- Cashless operations reduce theft risk
- Digital audit trail
- Secure payment processing
- Transaction verification

## Coverage

The cashless parking system is deployed across:
- Street parking zones
- Compound parking areas
- Public facility parking
- Event venue parking
