## Overview

**BECO2R** (Building Energy & Carbon Reporting) is Putrajaya's dedicated platform for tracking, reporting, and benchmarking energy consumption and carbon emissions from non-residential buildings.

## Purpose

To support Putrajaya's commitment to environmental sustainability by:
- Monitoring building energy performance
- Tracking carbon emissions
- Enabling data-driven efficiency improvements
- Supporting climate action goals

## Features

### Energy Reporting
- Monthly energy consumption tracking
- Multiple energy source monitoring
- Historical data analysis
- Trend visualization

### Carbon Calculation
- Automated CO2 emissions calculation
- Scope 1 and Scope 2 emissions
- Industry-standard methodologies
- Verification support

### Benchmarking
- Building-to-building comparison
- Industry benchmarks
- Performance ratings
- Best practice identification

## Platform Access

### Web Portal
- Comprehensive dashboard
- Detailed reporting tools
- Data export capabilities
- Administrative functions

### Mobile App
- On-the-go data entry
- Quick status checks
- Push notifications
- Simplified reporting

## Compliance

Supports compliance with:
- Putrajaya Green City requirements
- National energy efficiency targets
- Corporate sustainability reporting
- Environmental certifications

## Benefits

### For Building Owners
- Identify efficiency opportunities
- Track improvement progress
- Reduce operational costs
- Meet compliance requirements

### For the City
- City-wide emissions tracking
- Policy effectiveness measurement
- Climate action monitoring
- Green city certification support

## Data Security

- Secure data transmission
- User access controls
