## Overview

**Putrajaya Open Innovation (POINT)** is a community-driven innovation hub dedicated to fostering technology awareness, supporting local innovators, and building a vibrant ecosystem for smart city development.

## Mission

To inspire and empower Putrajaya's community, especially youth, to embrace technology and innovation as tools for creating a better future.

## Programs & Activities

### Technology Education
- IoT workshops and bootcamps
- Smart city technology seminars
- Coding and programming classes
- Maker space activities

### Youth Engagement
- School outreach programs
- Tech talent development
- Innovation competitions
- Mentorship programs

### Innovation Support
- Startup incubation support
- Prototype development assistance
- Technology testing facilities
- Network and partnership access

## Facilities

### Physical Space
- Co-working areas
- Workshop rooms
- Meeting facilities
- Demo and exhibition space

### Technical Resources
- Ubuntu mirror site (point.putrajaya.my)
- Development tools and equipment
- IoT sensor kits
- Prototyping materials

## Key Initiatives

### Ubuntu Mirror
- Local software repository
- Faster downloads for Malaysian users
- Open source promotion
- Developer community support

### Smart City Labs
- Hands-on technology experience
- Real-world problem solving
- Prototype testing
- Community feedback

## Partnerships

- Educational institutions
- Technology companies
- Government agencies
- Community organizations
- International networks

## Impact

- Youth technology literacy improvement
- Local innovation capacity building
- Smart city solution development
- Community engagement in city planning
