## Overview

The **EV Charging Station Network** is a strategic initiative to establish comprehensive electric vehicle charging infrastructure across Putrajaya. Through partnerships with leading energy providers including **Gentari** and **Tenaga Nasional Berhad (TNB)**, this project supports the nation's transition to sustainable transportation.

## Strategic Importance

### National EV Agenda

This project aligns with Malaysia's national goals:

- **Low Carbon Mobility Blueprint**: Supporting government EV targets
- **Carbon Neutrality**: Contributing to 2050 net-zero ambitions
- **Green City**: Positioning Putrajaya as a sustainable city model
- **Technology Leadership**: Showcasing smart charging infrastructure

### Putrajaya as EV Hub

As the federal administrative capital, Putrajaya serves as:

- Model city for EV adoption
- Testing ground for new charging technologies
- Showcase for visiting dignitaries and tourists
- Blueprint for other Malaysian cities

## Charging Infrastructure

### Types of Chargers

| Charger Type | Power | Charging Time | Use Case |
|--------------|-------|---------------|----------|
| DC Fast Charger | 50-150 kW | 20-40 mins | Quick top-up |
| AC Type 2 | 7-22 kW | 2-4 hours | Destination charging |
| AC Type 1 | 3.7 kW | 6-8 hours | Overnight charging |

Charging stations are strategically placed at:

- Shopping centers

## How to Use

### First-Time Users

1. **Download App**: Get Gentari/TNB charging app
2. **Register**: Create account and add payment
3. **Locate**: Find nearest available charger
4. **Navigate**: Get directions to station
5. **Charge**: Plug in and start session via app
6. **Pay**: Automatic payment on session end

### Tips for Best Experience

- Check availability before arriving
- Bring appropriate charging cable (if needed)
- Follow parking time limits
- Report any issues via app
