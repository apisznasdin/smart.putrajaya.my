## Overview

The **Smart CCTV & Panic Button System** represents a comprehensive public safety initiative that combines advanced artificial intelligence-powered surveillance technology with strategically placed emergency response mechanisms throughout Putrajaya.

## System Components

### Smart CCTV Network

The surveillance network comprises high-definition cameras equipped with intelligent video analytics capabilities:

- **Coverage**: Strategic placement across public areas, government precincts, residential zones, and commercial districts
- **Resolution**: Full HD and 4K cameras for clear image capture
- **Analytics**: AI-powered detection for unusual activities, crowd monitoring, and object tracking
- **Storage**: Cloud-based and on-premise storage with redundancy

### Panic Button Infrastructure

Emergency panic buttons are installed at key locations to provide immediate access to help:

- **Strategic Placement**: Bus stops, parks, pedestrian walkways, and public facilities
- **Instant Activation**: One-touch emergency alert system
- **Two-Way Communication**: Direct audio link to the Command Centre
- **GPS Location**: Automatic location transmission to responders

## AI-Powered Features

### Intelligent Video Analytics
- **Intrusion Detection**: Automatic alerts for unauthorized access to restricted areas
- **Crowd Analysis**: Real-time monitoring of crowd density and movement patterns
- **Behaviour Recognition**: Detection of suspicious activities or behaviours
- **Object Detection**: Identification of abandoned objects or unusual items

### Facial Recognition
- **Person of Interest**: Matching against authorized databases
- **Missing Persons**: Alert system for locating missing individuals
- **Access Control**: Integration with building security systems

### License Plate Recognition (LPR)
- **Vehicle Tracking**: Real-time vehicle movement monitoring
- **Stolen Vehicle Detection**: Automatic alerts for flagged vehicles
- **Traffic Enforcement**: Support for traffic violation detection

## Emergency Response Integration

The system is fully integrated with the Putrajaya Command Centre (PCC) for seamless emergency response:

1. **Alert Triggered**: Panic button pressed or AI detects incident
2. **Instant Notification**: Real-time alert sent to PCC operators
3. **Visual Verification**: Live camera feed accessed immediately
4. **Response Dispatch**: Appropriate emergency services deployed
5. **Incident Tracking**: Full documentation and follow-up

## Privacy & Data Protection

Perbadanan Putrajaya is committed to protecting citizen privacy:

- Compliance with Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA)
- Strict access controls and audit trails
- Data retention policies aligned with legal requirements
- Regular security assessments and updates

## Impact & Statistics

Since implementation, the system has contributed to:

- **Reduced Crime Rate**: Significant decrease in street crime incidents
- **Faster Response**: Average response time improved by 40%
- **Community Confidence**: Increased sense of safety among residents
- **Incident Resolution**: Higher rate of successful case investigations

## Community Engagement

Public awareness programs ensure residents understand:
- Location of panic buttons in their area
- How to use the emergency system effectively
- Privacy protections in place
- Their role in maintaining community safety
