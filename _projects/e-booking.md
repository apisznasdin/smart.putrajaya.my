## Overview

The **Online Facilities Booking** system (e-Tempahan) enables residents and organizations to easily check availability and reserve community halls, sports facilities, and other public venues through a convenient digital platform.

## Available Facilities

### Community Halls
- Dewan Komuniti (Community Halls)
- Multi-purpose halls
- Meeting rooms
- Event spaces

### Sports Facilities
- Badminton courts
- Futsal courts
- Swimming complexes
- Sports fields

## Features

### For Users
- Browse facility listings
- Check real-time availability
- Make online reservations
- Process payments digitally
- Receive instant confirmations
- Manage bookings

### For Management
- Calendar management
- Booking administration
- Revenue tracking
- Facility scheduling
- Report generation

## Booking Process

1. **Browse** - View available facilities
2. **Select** - Choose date and time
3. **Verify** - Check availability
4. **Reserve** - Make booking
5. **Pay** - Complete payment
6. **Confirm** - Receive confirmation

## Benefits

### Convenience
- 24/7 booking access
- No physical visit required
- Instant confirmation
- Easy rescheduling

### Efficiency
- Automated scheduling
- Reduced conflicts
- Better utilization
- Streamlined operations
