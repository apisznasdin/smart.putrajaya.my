## Overview

The **Variable Message Sign (VMS)** network is a critical component of Putrajaya's Public Information System (PIS), providing real-time traffic and safety information to road users through strategically placed LED displays.

## Functions

### Traffic Information
- Real-time congestion alerts
- Accident notifications
- Road closure information
- Alternative route suggestions

### Safety Messages
- Weather warnings
- Emergency alerts
- Public safety announcements
- Event-related advisories

### Public Information
- Event schedules
- City announcements
- Tourism information
- Service notifications

## Technical Specifications

- High-visibility LED technology
- Remote content management
- Weather-resistant enclosures
- 24/7 operation capability
- Centralized control from PCC

## Strategic Locations

VMS units are positioned at:
- Major highway entry points
- Key intersections
- Event venue approaches
- Government complex areas

## Integration

The VMS network is integrated with:
- Putrajaya Command Centre (PCC)
- Traffic management systems
- Emergency response protocols
- Event management coordination
