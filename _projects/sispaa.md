## Overview

**SISPAA** (Sistem Pengurusan Aduan Awam) is Putrajaya's centralized public complaint management system, providing citizens with a transparent and efficient channel to report issues and track their resolution.

## How It Works

### Submit Complaints
1. Access the portal at ppj.spab.gov.my
2. Select complaint category
3. Provide details and location
4. Attach supporting photos
5. Submit and receive reference number

### Track Progress
- Real-time status updates
- Department assignment visibility
- Resolution timeline
- Feedback mechanism

## Complaint Categories

- Infrastructure maintenance
- Public facilities
- Environmental issues
- Street lighting
- Road conditions
- Drainage problems
- Public cleanliness
- Other municipal services
