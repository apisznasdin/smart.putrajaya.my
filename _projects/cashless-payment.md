## Overview

The **Cashless Payment Society** initiative transforms Putrajaya into a digital payment-friendly city where residents and visitors can transact seamlessly using e-wallets and digital payment methods. This initiative extends beyond large retailers to include micro businesses, food stalls, and small vendors.

## Vision

To create an inclusive digital economy where every business, regardless of size, can accept digital payments, and every citizen can participate in the cashless ecosystem.

## Supported Payment Methods

### E-Wallets

Putrajaya businesses widely accept major e-wallet platforms:

| E-Wallet | Provider | Features |
|----------|----------|----------|
| DuitNow QR | National | Universal QR standard |
| Touch 'n Go eWallet | TNG Digital | Widely used, toll integration |
| GrabPay | Grab | Food delivery ecosystem |
| MAE | Maybank | Banking integration |
| Boost | Axiata | Rewards program |
| ShopeePay | Shopee | E-commerce integration |

### DuitNow QR

The **DuitNow QR** national standard enables:
- Single QR code for multiple e-wallets
- Interoperability across payment platforms
- Simplified merchant onboarding
- Standardized transaction processing

## Micro Business Enablement

### Supporting Small Vendors

A key focus is enabling micro businesses:

- **Street Food Vendors**: Hawkers and food stalls
- **Small Retailers**: Convenience stores, mini markets
- **Service Providers**: Home-based businesses
- **Market Traders**: Farmers' market sellers

### Onboarding Support

Perbadanan Putrajaya facilitates adoption through:

- Free merchant registration assistance
- QR code standee provision
- Training on e-wallet usage
- Technical support helpline

## Implementation

### Coverage Areas

Cashless payment acceptance spans:

- **Government Premises**: Counters, facilities, parking
- **Commercial Areas**: Shops, restaurants, services
- **Markets**: Wet markets, night markets
- **Events**: Festivals, exhibitions, gatherings
- **Transport**: Parking, public transit

### Business Registration

Simple onboarding process:

1. **Application**: Register with e-wallet provider
2. **Verification**: Identity and business validation
3. **Setup**: Receive QR code and materials
4. **Training**: Learn to accept payments
5. **Go Live**: Start accepting digital payments

## Benefits

### For Consumers

- **Convenience**: No need to carry cash
- **Safety**: Reduced theft and loss risk
- **Rewards**: Cashback and promotions
- **Records**: Digital transaction history
- **Hygiene**: Contactless payments

### For Businesses

- **No Cash Handling**: Reduced counting and banking
- **Instant Settlement**: Fast access to funds
- **Customer Base**: Attract digital-savvy customers
- **Lower Costs**: Reduce cash management expenses
- **Analytics**: Digital sales insights

### For the City

- **Modern Image**: Digital-ready city
- **Economic Activity**: Increased transactions
- **Financial Inclusion**: Bring more into formal economy
- **Transparency**: Traceable transactions
- **Efficiency**: Reduced cash logistics

## How to Participate

### For Businesses

1. Contact any supported e-wallet provider
2. Complete merchant registration
3. Receive your QR code
4. Display prominently
5. Start accepting payments

### For Consumers

1. Download preferred e-wallet app
2. Register and verify account
3. Top up or link payment method
4. Scan QR to pay
5. Enjoy cashless convenience
