## Overview

The **Putrajaya Urban Observatory (PUO)** is a comprehensive digital platform that serves as the Local Urban Observatory under the Malaysia Urban Observatory framework. It provides data-driven insights to support evidence-based urban planning and sustainable development in Putrajaya.

## Purpose & Vision

### Mission
To collect, analyse, and disseminate urban data that supports informed decision-making, promotes transparency, and enables sustainable urban development aligned with national and global goals.

### Vision
To establish Putrajaya as a model smart city with data-driven governance and citizen-centric urban planning.

## Key Functions

### Urban Data Collection
The PUO aggregates data from multiple sources:
- **City Systems**: Traffic, utilities, environment sensors
- **Government Agencies**: Population, economy, social services
- **Survey Data**: Citizen satisfaction, quality of life indicators
- **Open Sources**: Satellite imagery, public datasets

### Data Analytics & Visualization
- **Interactive Dashboards**: Real-time visualization of key urban metrics
- **Trend Analysis**: Historical data comparison and forecasting
- **Spatial Analysis**: GIS-based mapping and geographic insights
- **Custom Reports**: Tailored analytics for specific planning needs

### SDG Monitoring
The PUO tracks Putrajaya's progress towards the UN Sustainable Development Goals:
- Goal-by-goal performance indicators
- Localized SDG targets
- Progress tracking and reporting
- Benchmarking against national targets

## Platform Features

### Public Portal
Accessible at [puo.ppj.gov.my](https://puo.ppj.gov.my/), the public portal offers:
- **Open Data**: Downloadable datasets for researchers and public
- **Visualizations**: Interactive charts and maps
- **City Profile**: Comprehensive overview of Putrajaya's urban indicators
- **SDG Dashboard**: Progress on sustainable development goals

### Urban Indicators
The observatory monitors key indicators across domains:

| Domain | Sample Indicators |
|--------|-------------------|
| Population | Demographics, density, growth |
| Economy | Employment, business activity |
| Environment | Air quality, green spaces, waste |
| Infrastructure | Utilities, connectivity, transport |
| Governance | Service delivery, citizen satisfaction |
| Social | Education, health, safety |

### Data Integration
The PUO connects with various data sources:
- Smart city sensors and IoT devices
- Government administrative systems
- Survey and research data
- External data providers
