## Overview

The **Putrajaya NEDO EV Bus** represents a landmark achievement in Malaysia's sustainable transportation journey. As **Malaysia's first rapid-charge electric bus**, this project demonstrates Putrajaya's commitment to green mobility and positions the city as a pioneer in electric public transport.

## Project Background

### NEDO Partnership

This project was developed through collaboration with **Japan's New Energy and Industrial Technology Development Organization (NEDO)**:

- **Objective**: Demonstrate electric bus viability in tropical climate
- **Technology Transfer**: Japanese rapid-charging expertise
- **Research**: Data collection on EV bus performance
- **Capacity Building**: Local technical knowledge development

### Malaysian Production

The buses are produced locally by **DRB-HICOM's Defence Technologies (Deftech)**:

- First Malaysian-assembled electric bus
- Technology localization
- Local supply chain development
- Technical capability building
