# SDG Icons

This folder should contain the UN Sustainable Development Goals icons.

## Required Files

Download the official SDG icons from the UN and place them here with the following naming convention:

- `sdg-01.jpg` - No Poverty
- `sdg-02.jpg` - Zero Hunger
- `sdg-03.jpg` - Good Health & Well-being
- `sdg-04.jpg` - Quality Education
- `sdg-05.jpg` - Gender Equality
- `sdg-06.jpg` - Clean Water & Sanitation
- `sdg-07.jpg` - Affordable & Clean Energy
- `sdg-08.jpg` - Decent Work & Economic Growth
- `sdg-09.jpg` - Industry, Innovation & Infrastructure
- `sdg-10.jpg` - Reduced Inequalities
- `sdg-11.jpg` - Sustainable Cities & Communities
- `sdg-12.jpg` - Responsible Consumption & Production
- `sdg-13.jpg` - Climate Action
- `sdg-14.jpg` - Life Below Water
- `sdg-15.jpg` - Life on Land
- `sdg-16.jpg` - Peace, Justice & Strong Institutions
- `sdg-17.jpg` - Partnerships for the Goals

## Download Source

Official UN SDG icons can be downloaded from:
- https://www.un.org/sustainabledevelopment/news/communications-material/
- https://globalgoals.org/resources/

## File Format

- Format: JPG or PNG (rename to .jpg)
- Recommended size: 200x200px or larger (will be scaled by CSS)
