Deputy Prime Minister Datuk Seri Fadillah Yusof commended the extensive implementation of cashless payment systems at Putrajaya's Ramadan bazaars, highlighting it as a successful step towards a cashless society.

The successful deployment of cashless systems at the bazaar demonstrates Putrajaya's commitment to digital transformation and serves as a model for other cities to follow.
