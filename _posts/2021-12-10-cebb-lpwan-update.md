Updates on the installation of digital information boards and the deployment of a Low Power Wide Area Network (LPWAN) to support IoT device data transmission across the city.

The CEBB project enhances public communication while the LPWAN infrastructure provides the backbone for connecting thousands of IoT sensors throughout Putrajaya, enabling real-time monitoring and data collection.
