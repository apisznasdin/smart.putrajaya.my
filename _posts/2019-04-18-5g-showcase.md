During the 5G Malaysia Showcase in Putrajaya, then-PM Tun Dr. Mahathir Mohamad emphasized the government's commitment to 5G implementation to transform the national economy.

Putrajaya was chosen as the venue for this landmark event, showcasing various 5G use cases and demonstrating the transformative potential of next-generation connectivity.
