The Ministry of Federal Territories launched the "MySmart Wilayah 2030" blueprint, outlining 25 policies to guide the digital transformation of Putrajaya, Kuala Lumpur, and Labuan.

This comprehensive blueprint establishes a clear roadmap for smart city development across all Federal Territories, with specific initiatives tailored to each city's unique characteristics and needs.
