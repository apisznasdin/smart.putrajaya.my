The Putrajaya Urban Observatory (PUO) system was recognized with the "Smart City Award" at the Malaysian Institute of Planners (MIP) Excellence Awards 2024 for its data-driven city management capabilities.

This prestigious recognition highlights Putrajaya's leadership in urban data analytics and smart city planning, demonstrating the effectiveness of data-driven approaches to city management.
