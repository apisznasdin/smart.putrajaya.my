Digi Telecommunications demonstrated real-time 5G use cases in Putrajaya, including 5G-connected ambulances and augmented reality learning, as part of the national 5G showcase.

These demonstrations highlighted the practical applications of 5G technology in emergency services and education, paving the way for future smart city implementations.
