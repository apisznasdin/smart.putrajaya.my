PPj President Datuk Dr. Aminuddin Hassim outlined that the "Smart City, Green City, and Sports City" concepts would be the core drivers of Putrajaya's development for the 2020–2025 period.

These three interconnected concepts form the foundation of Putrajaya's holistic approach to urban development, balancing technological advancement with environmental sustainability and active lifestyle promotion.
