The upgraded Putrajaya Command Centre (PCC) was launched, operating 24/7 with high-definition CCTVs and panic buttons to monitor safety, traffic, and infrastructure city-wide.

This state-of-the-art facility serves as the nerve center for Putrajaya's smart city operations, enabling real-time monitoring and rapid response to any incidents or emergencies.
