A pilot run for the Automated Rapid Transit (ART) trackless tram was launched in Putrajaya, offering free rides to the public to test the viability of this smart, green transport solution.

The ART system represents a innovative approach to public transportation, combining the benefits of tram systems with the flexibility of bus routes, powered by clean energy technology.
