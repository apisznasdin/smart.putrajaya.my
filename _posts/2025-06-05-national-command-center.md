The Digital Ministry announced plans to establish a national smart city command center to integrate state-level initiatives, with Putrajaya serving as a key model for this centralized urban management system.

This initiative aims to create a unified platform for managing smart city operations across Malaysia, leveraging Putrajaya's experience as the administrative capital and pioneer in smart city development.
