Perbadanan Putrajaya partnered with Vectolabs Technologies to set up Malaysia's first municipal-operated IoT infrastructure using LoRa technology to manage smart street lighting and other assets.

This groundbreaking initiative makes Putrajaya the first municipality in Malaysia to own and operate its own IoT network, reducing dependency on external providers and ensuring data sovereignty.
