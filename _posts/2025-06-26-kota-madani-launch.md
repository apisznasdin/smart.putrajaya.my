Prime Minister Datuk Seri Anwar Ibrahim launched "Kota MADANI," a new RM4 billion smart city precinct in Putrajaya designed to be car-free at ground level and equipped with advanced digital infrastructure.

This landmark project represents a significant investment in Putrajaya's future as a smart city, featuring pedestrian-friendly design principles and cutting-edge technology integration throughout the development.
