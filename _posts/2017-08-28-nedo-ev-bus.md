Japan's NEDO and Putrajaya Corporation launched a demonstration project for a Super Quick Charge Electric Vehicle (EV) bus system to replace diesel buses in the city.

This pioneering project demonstrated the viability of electric public transportation in tropical climates and contributed valuable insights for Putrajaya's green mobility initiatives.
