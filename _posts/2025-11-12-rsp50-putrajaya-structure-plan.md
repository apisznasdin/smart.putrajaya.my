Putrajaya Corporation (PPj) is currently formulating the "Putrajaya Structure Plan 2050" (RSP50) to guide the city's development strategies and transform it into a "Visionary Smart City" for the next 25 years.

This comprehensive plan will establish the framework for Putrajaya's transformation into a leading smart city, incorporating sustainable development principles, advanced technology integration, and enhanced quality of life for all residents.
