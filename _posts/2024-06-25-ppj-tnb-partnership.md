PPj and Tenaga Nasional Bhd (TNB) signed a strategic deal to transform the administrative capital into a Smart City, focusing on renewable energy and energy efficiency as part of the Smart City Action Plan.

This partnership marks a significant milestone in Putrajaya's journey towards sustainable energy management and positions the city as a leader in green smart city initiatives.
