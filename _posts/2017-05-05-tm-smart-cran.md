Telekom Malaysia (TM) signed agreements with PPj to deploy "Smart C-RAN" (Centralised Radio Access Network) infrastructure, a key foundation for the city's future mobile and smart connectivity.

This agreement marked an important milestone in establishing the advanced telecommunications infrastructure required to support Putrajaya's smart city vision.
