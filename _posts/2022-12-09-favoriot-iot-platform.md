PPj signed a collaboration agreement with Favoriot Sdn Bhd to establish a municipal-operated Smart City IoT platform, integrating multi-vendor sensors into a single dashboard at the Putrajaya Command Centre.

This partnership enables Putrajaya to consolidate data from various IoT sensors and devices across the city, providing a unified view for better decision-making and resource management.
