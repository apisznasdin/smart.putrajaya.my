Core Framework

The 7 Smart Domains

Our comprehensive approach to smart city development covers seven interconnected domains, each designed to enhance the quality of life for Putrajaya's residents.

Smart Transportation & Mobility

Integrated mobility solutions including autonomous vehicles, micro-mobility services, and intelligent traffic management.

Smart Home & Environment

Sustainable living through environmental monitoring, green initiatives, and smart home integration.

Smart Government Services

Digital-first government services enabling seamless citizen engagement and efficient public administration.

Smart Infrastructure & Utilities

Connected infrastructure with smart grid systems, IoT sensors, and optimized utility management.

Smart Safety & Security

AI-powered surveillance, emergency response systems, and public safety innovations.

Smart Economy

Digital economy enablement through innovation hubs, startup ecosystem, and smart business solutions.

Smart Community

Citizen-centric programs fostering digital literacy, community engagement, and inclusive development.
